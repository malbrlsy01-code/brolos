About Sana

Sana Tower covers an area of 46,000 square meters, with a beachfront frontage of 450-500 meters and a rear frontage of 300 meters overlooking Zahia Villas. It includes three towers: two towers (commercial and residential) and one hotel tower. Commercial spaces range from 18 to 450 square meters, and residential spaces range from 53 to 183 square meters. A beachfront plot of 25,000 square meters with a 250-meter shoreline is also included.
